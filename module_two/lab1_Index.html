About Raiz

What began as a simple passion for working with timber has grown into a craft that fills homes with warmth, beauty, and meaning. At Raiz, every plate, mug, bowl, or table starts with a raw piece of wood — pine, walnut, jarrah, elm, red gum, elderbox, rose mahogany, silky oak, blue gum, or blackwood — each chosen for its unique grain and character. I shape them by hand, using traditional woodturning and woodcraft techniques, and often pair them with flowing resin designs that capture the elegance of nature. Over time, I have expanded my creations to include cap holders, book stands, phone holders, boxes, chopping boards, napkin holders, and coasters, all designed to be as functional as they are beautiful. 

Every piece carries the story of the tree it came from and the hours of care put into crafting it, making it more than just an object — it becomes part of your home’s story too.
